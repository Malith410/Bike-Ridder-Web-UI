Riding Tips for Sri Lanka

Lessons learned from thousands of kilometers on Sri Lankan roads

Monsoon Preparedness

The southwest monsoon (May-September) and northeast monsoon (November-February) require different preparation. Always pack waterproof gear and be extra cautious on wet roads, especially in hill country.

Route Planning

Google Maps doesn't always show road conditions accurately. Check with locals about road conditions, especially when planning mountain routes. The A-B roads often offer more scenic (and challenging) alternatives to highways.

Mechanical Know-How

Learn basic maintenance - how to fix a flat, adjust your chain, and check oil levels. While there are mechanics in most towns, being self-sufficient in remote areas is invaluable. Always carry a basic toolkit.
